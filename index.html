Biometric Scanner Installation Guide

Integrate physical hardware such as the SecuGen Hamster Pro 20 fingerprint machine directly into your web browser.

1

Device Connection

Connect your SecuGen Hamster Pro 20 USB scanner into your machine's physical USB port. Ensure the red biometric scanner optical prism lights up.

2

Driver & Service Installation

Install SecuGen's official WebAPI background daemon service onto your local host server. This exposes biometric templates directly on your secure local port.

3

Localhost SSL Certification

Your browser blocks connection to insecure local addresses. Open https://localhost:8443/SGIFPM_STATUS in a separate tab, and accept the self-signed SSL/TLS certification to grant local bridge permissions.
